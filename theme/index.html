What Do We Offer

We buy homes in North Carolina! At Expand Estates LLC, we will buy your home in any condition regardless of your credit score. We guarantee a fair cash offer for your property and you can have cash in hand and be free of your unwanted property in as little as one week!

Fast Closing

We operate very quickly! We understand the importance of liquidating cash. Fast closing means cash will be in your bank accounts sooner rather than later.

All Cash

We pay all cash upfront! That means no waiting for loan approvals, no fees or financial contingenies, and an overall simplified process!

24 Hour Customer Support

Throughout the closing process, 24 Hour support will be available to answer any questions that arise.

Secure System

By using a trusted attorney, we can have developed a system to ensure quick and secure Title Transfers as well as cash transfers.
