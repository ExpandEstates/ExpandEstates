How It Works

Call, text, email, or fill out the form!

Contact Us

Whether you prefer a quick chat or filling out our convenient online form, getting started is easy. Dial (704) 659-6832 or access our form to receive a cash offer for your home. Once we receive your information, we'll carefully assess your property details and arrange a convenient meeting time at your location.

Home Walk-Through

We'll accompany you on a brief walk-through of your property, assessing any necessary repairs along the way. Rest assured, you won't need to worry about handling any repairs yourself – we'll take care of everything for you. After the walk-through, we'll proceed to sign a purchase agreement with you.

Get Paid Fast!

You have the flexibility to choose the closing date that suits you best. Whether you prefer a quick closing or need more time, we'll accommodate your schedule. Simply show up, sign the paperwork, and the title company will promptly wire your funds within a few hours. It's that simple – we'll handle the rest!

Simple To Start

This means no worrying about extra expenses or having to prepare your house for sale – we'll purchase it as-is. You can forget about the hassle of repairs or cleaning up your property.
